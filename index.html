Overview

The Dell UltraSharp U2720Q is a 27-inch 4K UHD (3840 x 2160) IPS monitor designed for content creators, photographers, and professionals who demand color accuracy and stunning visuals. With 99% sRGB color gamut coverage and HDR10 support, it delivers vibrant, lifelike images.

Key highlights include a sleek design, multiple connectivity options, and ergonomic adjustments for all-day comfort.

Key Features

4K UHD Resolution

Enjoy crisp, detailed visuals with 3840 x 2160 pixels, perfect for high-resolution tasks like video editing and graphic design.

Color Accuracy

99% sRGB and 95% DCI-P3 coverage ensure true-to-life colors, calibrated for professional use.

Ergonomic Design

Adjustable height, tilt, swivel, and pivot for optimal viewing angles and reduced eye strain.
